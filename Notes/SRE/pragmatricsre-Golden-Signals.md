## The 4 golden Signals

## 1.  Latency:
1. This measures the time it takes to service a request.
2. Low latency is crucial for a good user experience.

## 2. Traffic:

1.  This measures the volume of requests being processed by the system.
2.  Monitoring traffic helps identify trends and potential bottlenecks.

## 3.  Errors:

1.  This measures the rate of failed requests.
2.  A high error rate indicates potential problems with the system.

## 4. Saturation:

1.  This measures the resource utilization of the system, such as CPU, memory, and disk usage.
2.  High saturation can lead to performance degradation and failures.

##  By monitoring these four metrics, SREs can gain a comprehensive understanding of their system's health.

1.  identify issues early
2.  troubleshoot problems effectively
3.  optimize system performance.
4.  these metrics can be used to establish Service Level Indicators (SLIs)
5.  and Service Level Objectives (SLOs), which are key components of SRE practices
